Hi! Nice to see you here!

I am an economics PhD student at the Technical University of Munich, working on behavioral and labor market topics. I started my PhD under the supervision of Philipp Lergetporer in October 2021 and plan to complete in September 2025. For my single-authored project "Stormproof and Stable? The Effects of Resilience-Building Exercises on Adolescents Aboard", I received the Add-On Fellowship by the Joachim Herz Foundation (2022-2025). Under guidance of Stephan Maurer, I will be a visiting researcher at the University of Edinburgh in September / October 2024.
